Vision

An empowered generation with physical and life skills that enable children effectively face and handle the challenges of the millennium.

Mission

To create awareness for physical fitness among children as obligation and not an option.

Goal

We aim to increase youth, especially female, participation in sports and fitness through a focus on health literacy, physical education, adaptive sports, cradle games, and event management.

STRATEGY

We believe that children ought to be given an opportunity to develop and exercise their abilities in sports. We do train, mentor, equip and develop children in various disciplines through clubs like Table tennis, chess, badminton, basketball and football clubs etc with special emphasis given to both boys and girls from the age of 3 to 16 years. During the training sessions children are clustered according to their age groups. We focus on equipping them with the basic skills of that particular sport and also expose them to national and international tournaments.

Note: Charges and calendar can always be discussed.

Programs and Activities

Football Training Program

Our comprehensive football training program is designed to develop players of all skill levels. From fundamental techniques to advanced strategies, we cover it all. Join us to elevate your game to new heights.

Youth Soccer League

Engage in friendly competition and teamwork through our youth soccer league. It's not just a game; it's an opportunity for your child to build lasting friendships and sportsmanship skills.

Fitness and Conditioning

Our fitness and conditioning sessions focus on developing the physical strength, agility, and endurance needed for peak performance on the field. Train with us to stay in top shape.
