Recommendations

Benefits of Our Fitness Programme

Government education and health departments have standards and recommendations in place to ensure all children are provided the opportunity to participate in physical education literacy sessions, but these are rarely met due to the absence of specialist PE instructors in many of our communities. Children need the value and habit of lifelong physical activity to be instilled in them from an early age.

They deserve meaningful physical education literacy sessions that introduce a variety of movement experiences which set the foundation for lifelong physical activity and body fitness. This is important from a health, education, spiritual and social perspective and as a contributor to body immunity.

Physical education creates a platform for delivery of other social services important for self - preservation. If children experience positive and fun physical education sessions more frequently during early years, there seems a greater likely hood that they may live longer.

As instructors, we engage children and encourage them to value and enjoy physical education sessions. This in turn helps them see the connection between the benefits of participating in physical activity and the positive impact this has on their lives.

Safety

We put safety of our participants at the forefront during and after the coaching sessions.

Challenges

"The physical fitness literacy" social action program is a key element in our projects because we are aware that communities all over the world experience challenges such as:

  1. Health issues HIV/AIDS, obesity, drug abuse.
  2. Education issues - mental focus, resilience, team work and competitiveness.
  3. Social inclusion issues - discrimination, anti - social behavior, crime.
  4. Gender quality issues - do girls and woman have equal opportunities to play? equal opportunities for all demographics in the community
